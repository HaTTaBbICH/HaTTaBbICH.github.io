Why Choose CryptoCoin Widget?

In the highly volatile cryptocurrency market, checking prices by repeatedly opening an exchange app is inefficient. CryptoCoin Widget solves this by displaying live market data on your Android home screen. Designed for traders, HODLers, and crypto enthusiasts, our widget provides instant access to trading pairs, 24-hour volume changes, and interactive candlestick charts.

Core Capabilities:

  • Real-Time Sync: Uses robust WebSocket and REST APIs (Binance) to ensure zero delay in price feeds.
  • Customizable UI: Supports Glassmorphism, dark/light themes, custom chart colors, and adjustable opacity to match your wallpaper perfectly.
  • Multi-Coin Support: Monitor up to multiple assets in a single compact widget block.
  • Battery Efficient: Smart background sync. The widget updates on the lock screen and supports "Quiet Hours" to save battery at night.
