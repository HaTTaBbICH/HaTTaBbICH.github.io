Advanced Widget Features

Real-time Binance Sync

Direct integration with Binance REST API and WebSockets ensures you receive the most accurate and up-to-date pricing data without intermediary server delays.

Battery & Network Optimization

Intelligent background processing. The widget continues to update on the lock screen and supports 'Quiet Hours' to prevent battery drain during the night.

Glassmorphism UI Customization

Fully adjust the aesthetic of your widget. Control background opacity, corner radius, border width, and dynamic neon glow effects to match any wallpaper.

Multi-Coin & Portfolio Modes

Track a single asset with a detailed candlestick chart, or monitor up to 4 different coins simultaneously in a compact multi-coin list layout.
