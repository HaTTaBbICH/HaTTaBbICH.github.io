Advanced Widget Features

Real-time Binance Sync

Direct integration with Binance REST API and WebSockets ensures you receive the most accurate pricing data without intermediary delays.

Battery & Network Optimization

Intelligent background processing. The widget continues to update on the lock screen and supports 'Quiet Hours' to save battery at night.

Glassmorphism UI Customization

Fully adjust the aesthetic of your widget. Control background opacity, corner radius, border width, and dynamic neon glow effects.

Multi-Coin & Portfolio Modes

Track a single asset with a detailed candlestick chart, or monitor up to 4 different coins simultaneously in a compact layout.
