Frequently Asked Questions

Does the widget drain battery?

No. The widget uses smart background sync via Android WorkManager. It updates on the lock screen, but you can enable 'Quiet Hours' to completely stop network requests during the night.

How do I change the crypto coin?

Tap the small gear (settings) icon in the corner of the widget. This opens the configuration menu where you can select a new trading pair and exchange.

Why is the widget not updating?

Check your internet connection. Also, ensure that battery optimization for the app is disabled in your Android system settings, as aggressive OS power management can kill background tasks.
