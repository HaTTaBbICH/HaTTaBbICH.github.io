Installation Guide

Adding the Widget to Your Home Screen

  1. Long-press on any empty space on your Android home screen.
  2. Tap the Widgets icon that appears at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Scroll down the list until you find CryptoCoin Widget.
  4. Choose your preferred widget size (Compact, Large Chart, Multi-Coin, etc.), tap and hold it, then drag it to your home screen.
  5. The configuration screen will open automatically. Select your trading pair, preferred exchange, and visual settings (transparency, colors).
  6. Tap Save. The widget will now fetch the latest data and display it on your screen.
